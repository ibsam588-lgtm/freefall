## Full description (max 4000 chars)

How deep can you fall?

Freefall is an infinite arcade falling game that puts gravity on your side
and danger everywhere else. Tilt your phone to steer, tap to dash, and
plunge through five hand-crafted zones — each one stranger and more
dangerous than the last.

FIVE ZONES, ONE LONG DROP
Start in the high stratosphere and chase the horizon down through the
neon city skyline, into the cracked underground, past the crushing
pressure of the deep ocean, and finally into the molten core. Every
zone changes the rules: new obstacles, new hazards, new music, new
ways to die spectacularly.

TILT + TOUCH CONTROLS
Pick up and play in seconds. Tilt to glide left and right, tap to
dash, hold to slow your descent. The controls are designed to feel
fluid the moment you start falling — and rewarding when you start
chaining moves.

COLLECT, COMBO, COMPETE
Snag coins on the way down. Stack pickups without taking a hit to
trigger combo multipliers — the longer your streak, the bigger the
payout. Spend coins in the in-game store on character skins and
particle trails to make every run look as good as it plays.

DAILY REWARDS
Log in every day for escalating bonuses. Miss a day and the streak
resets — so don't.

ACHIEVEMENTS & LEADERBOARDS
Climb the global leaderboards on Google Play Games and chase down
dozens of achievements. Bragging rights are earned, never given.

KEY FEATURES
- Infinite procedurally generated falling levels
- 5 distinct zones with their own art, music, and obstacles
- Tilt + touch controls tuned for one-handed play
- Coin pickups, combo multipliers, and a stylish in-game store
- Skins and particle trails to customize your fall
- Daily login rewards
- Achievements and global leaderboards
- Quick runs perfect for a single-minute play session
- No internet required — play anywhere

THE BEST RUNS ARE THE LONGEST ONES
Freefall is built around one question: how deep can you fall before
something stops you? Every wall you slip past, every coin you snag at
the last second, every zone you reach for the first time pushes that
number a little higher.

Strap in. Aim down. Don't look up.
